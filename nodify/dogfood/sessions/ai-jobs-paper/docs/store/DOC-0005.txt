--- ANALYSIS NOTE (not from source, for triage) ---
Both WEF reports are EMPLOYER-SURVEY PROJECTIONS (forward-looking, 2023-2027 and 2025-2030), not
realized/measured 2020-2025 outcomes. The 2023 report projects a NET NEGATIVE of 14 million jobs
overall (even though AI/ML specialist roles specifically grow +40%/+1M) — so "AI adoption creates
net new jobs" is true for the AI-specialist/data-specialist occupational category specifically, but
the WEF's own 2023 economy-wide net figure was negative (driven by macro factors, not AI per se).
The 2025 report reverses to a net positive of +78M for 2025-2030, again a projection, not measured
history.
