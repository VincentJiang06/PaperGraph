KEY FINDING: On Upwork (a real online freelance labor market), the release of ChatGPT (Nov 2022)
was followed by a measurable, realized decline in freelance writing-category demand: -2% number of
monthly jobs/contracts and -5% to -5.2% monthly earnings for exposed (writing/text) freelancers;
a near-identical pattern (-3.7% jobs, -9.4% earnings) hit image-related freelancers after DALL-E 2 /
Midjourney. Effects were concentrated in short-term, less complex jobs and (counter-intuitively)
were larger, not smaller, for previously top-rated/high-skill freelancers — because generative AI
let lower-skill freelancers approximate top-tier output quality, compressing the skill premium.
