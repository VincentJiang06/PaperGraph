SOURCE S1
Title: GPTs are GPTs: An Early Look at the Labor Market Impact Potential of Large Language Models
Authors: Tyna Eloundou, Sam Manning, Pamela Mishkin, Daniel Rock (2023)
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.10130
Fetched: 2026-07-09

VERBATIM KEY PASSAGES (from abstract):

"around 80% of the U.S. workforce could have at least 10% of their work tasks affected by the introduction of LLMs"

"approximately 19% of workers may see at least 50% of their tasks impacted"

"about 15% of all worker tasks in the US could be completed significantly faster at the same level of quality"

"When incorporating software and tooling built on top of LLMs, this share increases to between 47 and 56% of all tasks"

"LLMs such as GPTs exhibit traits of general-purpose technologies"
