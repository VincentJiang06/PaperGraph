SOURCE S4
Title: Generative AI at Work
Authors: Erik Brynjolfsson, Danielle Li, Lindsey R. Raymond (NBER Working Paper 31161, 2023; published QJE 2025)
URL: https://www.nber.org/papers/w31161
Fetched: 2026-07-09

VERBATIM KEY PASSAGES (from abstract):

"we study the staggered introduction of a generative AI-based conversational assistant using data from 5,179 customer support agents"

"Access to the tool increases productivity, as measured by issues resolved per hour, by 14% on average"

"including a 34% improvement for novice and low-skilled workers but with minimal impact on experienced and highly skilled workers"
