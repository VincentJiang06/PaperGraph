SOURCE S8
Title: Generative AI and jobs: A 2025 update
Author: International Labour Organization (ILO), with NASK, 2025.
URL: https://www.ilo.org/publications/generative-ai-and-jobs-2025-update

VERBATIM EXTRACTS (exposure vs transformation):

- "One in four workers across the world are in an occupation with some degree of GenAI exposure."
- "most jobs will be transformed rather than made redundant."
- The brief reports "a mean automation score of 0.29 in 2025 versus 0.30 in 2023," indicating slightly lower exposure than prior estimates.
- Growing GenAI capabilities in "voice, image and video generation" have increased exposure for media and web-related roles.
- Managing the transition requires "social dialogue, to enhance both working conditions and productivity."

Interpretation note: The ILO frames the effect as TRANSFORMATION of job content (task recomposition) rather than automatic ELIMINATION of jobs; exposure is not the same as displacement.
